Biography

Rui Zhang is pursuing his Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Prof. Song Guo. Before that, he has received his M.Eng. degree from Zhejiang University in 2020, co-supervised by Prof. Peng Cheng and Prof. Jiming Chen, and B.Eng. degree from Northeastern University in 2017.

His research interest broadly lies in the areas of Trustworthy Machine Learning, Federated Learning, and Differential Privacy. Some specific topics such as gradient inversion, label recovery, data auditing, and privacy-preserved learning are highly involved.
