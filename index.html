Biography

Rui Zhang is a first-year PhD student at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Prof. Song Guo (IEEE Fellow). His doctoral main research area includes Federated Learning, Differential Privacy, and privacy & security in Machine Learning. Before that, he received his M.E. degree in Control Science and Engineering from Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Jiming Chen (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Peng Cheng in Networked Sensing and Control (NeSC) group.
